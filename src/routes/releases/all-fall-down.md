Another banger by Asheville NC pop punk band Drowning Leo! The band tracked live at the studio, and then we overdubbed guitars and vocals. The band brought me on as a producer for this song, and we worked together to trim the fat and give the choruses maximum impact.

The lyrics to this one crystalized after the government sanctioned murders of American citizens Renée Good and Alex Pretti in Minneapolis, so the music is understandably a bit more somber in tone than many of the band's more upbeat pop punk leaning tracks.
